PREAMBLE

The µFlow software ("the Software") is a research instrument developed
at Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey for the modeling of
microfluidic Hall-effect magnetic bead detection. It comprises the
hosted browser application at https://uflow.studio (or such successor
URL as the Licensors may designate), the source code repository at
https://github.com/hg293/microfluidic-hall-sensor, the LaTeX manuscript
sources, generated figures, supporting bibliographic data, and any
